App Description :
This app is for adding members of Clubs of Dinajpur Govt City College. Through the app, members can be added to Science Club, English Language Club, Programming Club and Debate Club. Adding member information through the app will be saved directly in the discord app's respective club specific channel.

⚠️ Limitation : A total of 200 members can be added in 4 clubs with 50 members in each club per month.

Terms of use:

  • First open the app and ensure internet connection.
  • Four clubs are named inside the app. Click on the club you want to add a member to.
  • Then a form will appear fill the form with correct information.
  • Submit the form by verifying that all the information is correct.
